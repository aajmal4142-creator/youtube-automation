# Permanent Static Assets Folder

Put your royalty-free background music and transition sound effects in this folder!

The video builder is pre-configured to look for these files:
1. `background_viral.mp3` - Your emotional/dramatic background music track (will be looped and mixed at 12% volume).
2. `whoosh.mp3` - Your transition sound effect (will be automatically mixed at 80% volume at every clip transition).

### Recommended Free Sources:
1. **Pixabay Music (pixabay.com/music/)** - Search for "suspense dramatic emotional cinema" background tracks and download one as `background_viral.mp3`. Search for "whoosh" sound effects and download one as `whoosh.mp3`.
2. **YouTube Audio Library** - Download your favorite cinematic suspense themes and transition sound effects.

### Why put them here?
Unlike the `assets/` directory (which contains temporary scraped video files and is wiped clean on every run to save disk space), this `static_assets/` folder is **never cleaned up**, so your custom background branding will persist permanently!
